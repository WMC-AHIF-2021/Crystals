Dalmatian jasper, better known as Dalmatian stone, is typically found and mined in Chihuahua, Mexico

Dalmatian Jasper is known as a nurturing stone, with its ability to bring out the child within. It helps bring clarity of mind to reveal one's strengths and weaknesses; bringing in a calm sense of tranquility, wholeness, and control. This unique mineral helps one pursue their goals and provides the necessary tools needed along their journey.
