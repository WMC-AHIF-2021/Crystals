Smoky Quartz

Information

Smoky Quartz is a type of Quartz. This particular Quartz variety forms in masses and crystal structures of all sizes. It can be found all over the world where the best specimens have come from Africa, Australia, and the New England region of the United States.

Smoky quartz meaning signifies stability and grounding, so much so that the crystal is called the grounding stone. Smoky quartz is a variety from the quartz crystal family. Mirroring its smoky hue is smoky quartz spiritual meaning, which represents grounding, letting go, and surrendering old wounds.
