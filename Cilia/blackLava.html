Lava Stone is a grounding stone that strengthens one's connection to Mother Earth. It gives us strength and courage, allowing us stability through times of change. It can balance the emotions and bring about calmness and strength. It's believed to help keep tempers in check and also help to work through problems in a logical way.

Lava stone is said to alleviate anxiety, promote emotional tranquillity and bring calmness and feelings of relaxation.
